Typography

About

This website will present some famous traditional/historically significant typefaces and some experimental/modern ones. Points that make the font stand out will be highlighted for each. A design page using quotes will also be included; refer to illustration (a). Will illustrate ligatures, alternates and readability/legibility visually.

Audience

General people with no prior knowledge in typography

Design & Style

Design will correspond and complement font designs. To clearly show designs, contrasting colors will be used. Strong primary colors and its complementary colors may be used to emphasize. Color palette is subject to change, but will use vibrant colors like magenta and ultramarine (to minimum amounts as they tend to hurt your eyes). Background colors may include natural tan colors, white, black, and complementary colors to the selected vibrant colors. Since we are using typography, it will involve lots of text, so I'll make it so that the text is closer to images and designs rather than plain text. Utilize font weights, alignment, size, position, and angles.
